Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026
Important Safety Notice: Lighthouse does not guarantee safety. Route suggestions are provided for informational purposes only. Users should always stay aware of their surroundings, follow official guidance, and use personal judgment.

Overview

Lighthouse is a safety-aware navigation app designed to help users make more informed walking route decisions by analyzing route awareness, nearby open support points, main roads, and low-support sections.

Information We Use

Location Data

Lighthouse uses your current location to calculate walking routes, show your position on the map, analyze nearby open support points along your route, and provide navigation progress while the app is in use.

Location data is used only while you are using the app. Lighthouse does not store your precise location history on its own servers.

Search Queries

When you search for a destination, your search text may be used to return relevant place suggestions, addresses, and coordinates.

Route and Place Data

Lighthouse may process walking route details, selected destination data, place suggestions, nearby open places, and support point coverage to calculate route awareness.

Third-Party Services

Lighthouse uses Google Maps Platform services, including Google Maps, Google Places, and Google Routes, to provide maps, place search, route calculation, and nearby place information.

When these services are used, certain information such as your approximate location, selected destination, route request, or place search query may be processed by Google according to Google’s own privacy practices.

Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Storage

  • Lighthouse does not require a user account.
  • Lighthouse does not sell your personal data.
  • Lighthouse does not store your precise location history on its own servers.
  • Some preferences, such as selected language and onboarding status, may be stored locally on your device.

Analytics and Tracking

Lighthouse does not use third-party advertising tracking.

Children’s Privacy

Lighthouse is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Safety Notice

Lighthouse is an informational navigation support tool. It does not provide emergency services, does not replace official safety guidance, and does not guarantee that a route is safe.

In case of danger or emergency, contact local emergency services immediately.

Your Choices

You can disable location access at any time from your device settings:

Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Lighthouse
